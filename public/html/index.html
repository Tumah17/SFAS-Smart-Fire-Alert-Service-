Why this project matters

In Liberia and similar regions, fast notification and localized situational awareness save lives. Our devices are low-cost, solar-ready, and designed for local maintenance ‚Äî that means scalable protection across neighborhoods.

Deploy

Plug-in device, connect to Wi-Fi/LoRa gateway or local mesh, register using the household ID.

Monitor

Temperature, smoke density, and battery telemetry stream continuously to dashboards and the mobile app.

Act

Automated alerts guide first responders and neighbors; system logs enable after-action analysis.

Technical snapshot

Hardware

Sensors: smoke, temp, CO. MCU with fallback logging, RTC, low-power modes.

Connectivity

Wi-Fi + optional LoRa mesh for greater reach. Devices can work offline and sync later.

Cloud & Analytics

Secure MQTT ingestion, time-series DB, and analytics for trend detection and alerts.

Impact & deployment plan

Pilot neighborhoods in Monrovia ‚Üí expand to counties. Our target: 5,000 households in year-1 with priority on schools and markets.

Community training

Local maintenance teams and awareness workshops.

Partnerships

Coordination with fire services and local government.

Sustainability

Solar-capable units and low-cost replacement parts.
